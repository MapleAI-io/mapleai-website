About

Not an agency. A local operator.

Maple AI is based in Orangeville, Ontario. We work with small and mid-sized service businesses across Dufferin County and surrounding areas — building AI systems that actually get used.

Our Story

Built for the businesses GTA agencies ignore

Most AI consulting firms are built for enterprise. Their pricing assumes a legal team, a procurement process, and a budget line item that starts with a comma. That's not most businesses in Orangeville or Dufferin County.

Maple AI exists because local service businesses — trades, contractors, law firms, real estate, healthcare — are sitting on real automation opportunities and getting nothing from the market. Either the firms are too expensive, too generic, or too far away to actually understand what you need.

We're different because we live here. We know the local economy, we work with businesses your size, and we build things that fit how small teams actually operate — not how enterprise IT departments do.

The result is AI that gets used, not shelfware.

How We Work

What you can expect

Straight talk

If AI won't help you, we'll say so. We'd rather lose a project than overpromise. Every recommendation comes with an honest ROI estimate.

No hand-offs

You deal with the same person from the first call to the finished build. No account managers, no junior consultants, no project coordinators in the way.

Builds that get used

We design for adoption, not demos. If your team won't use it in week three, it's not a success. We measure by outcomes, not deliverables.

Local first

We're based in Orangeville and prioritize Dufferin County businesses. We understand the local economy and what's actually realistic for businesses here.

Plain language

No jargon, no technical overload. We explain what we're building and why in terms that make sense to someone running a business, not a software team.

Built to last

We build on platforms that will still be running in 3 years. No experimental tools, no fragile workarounds. Practical, stable, maintainable.
